# Provide Liquidity on Uniswap
-- How you can make your token buyable, for this purpose you have to create a pool 

Step 01: Go to https://uniswap.org/
Step 02: COnnect your wallet and account
Step 03: Select Pool option
Step 04: Follow screen shots given in directory.
Step 05: Swap your token with Ether.

Congratulations you are providing liquidity on uniswap.
