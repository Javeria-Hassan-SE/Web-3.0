# Create ERC-20 Token

Step 01: Go to https://cointool.app/dashboard 
Cointool is basicall a DAPP. 

Step 02: Scroll down to sidebar and select Create Token option in ETH.
Step 03: Connect you Wallet and account.
Step 04: Provide required information. Choose your Unique token name and its supply.
Step 05: Click on Create token, it will ask for confirmation and you have to wait for sometime.
Step 06: Copy your token link and address.

Congratulations! Your token has been created.
