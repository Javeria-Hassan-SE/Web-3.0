
# Getting Started with Metamask

Step 01: Install Metamask chrome Extension
Step 02: Create Wallet 
Note: Must remeber your seed phrase
Step 03: Create another account and use it for test transactions.
Step 04: Go to Settings and turn on "Show Test Networks" option.
Step 05: Go to https://goerli-faucet.pk910.de/ and https://goerlifaucet.com/
Step 06: Connect your wallet here
Step 07: Paste your wallet address and get some ether for test transactions purpose


Yup! you are ready to use your wallet across web 3.0
